Clothing & Body

These are template textures for making clothing. Some kits have a sculptmap texture for hoods, pants cuffs etc. Please see the Sculpts page for instructions on how to build the sculpt from the sculptmap texture. To make the clothing click the picture to download the zipped file. Unzip the file. In the folder you will find the template textures. You can take these to your graphics program and modify any way you like or you can upload them into the grid of your choice. Once in world go to your clothing folder in your inventory and right click. Choose the clothing item you would like to make and wear it. Then go to edit appearance on your avatar and click the tab that matches the clothing that you are making (shirt,pants etc.) Apply the texture and “save as”. Name it and you are done.
